Poison By Aris

Poison by Aris is a beautiful fragrance for women, which is a delicate floral blend of aromas. It hypnotizes and mesmerizes with a meticulous selection of ingredients. The perfume combines distinct notes to deliver a beautiful scent that is ideal for daily wear. This perfume is available in a 100ml bottle in the EDP concentration. The scent features aqueous, temperately sweet litchi, with a hint of tangy pomegranate blossom, and a subtle, refined nuance of water lily. All of which seamlessly settle on the very floral note of Chinese pink peony and fresh guava. Musk and various woody notes constitute the base on which the rest of your perfume pyramid lies.
